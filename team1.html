20-Day YouTube Shorts Plan for CCNA Basics

Day Title Description
Day 1 What is CCNA? Brief introduction to Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) and its importance in networking.
Day 2 Understanding Networks Basic concepts of networking, including types of networks and their uses.
Day 3 OSI Model Overview Introduction to the OSI model and its seven layers.
Day 4 Layer 1: Physical Layer Explanation of the Physical Layer, its functions, and components.
Day 5 Layer 2: Data Link Layer Overview of the Data Link Layer and its role in networking.
Day 6 Layer 3: Network Layer Introduction to the Network Layer and its functions.
Day 7 Layer 4: Transport Layer Explanation of the Transport Layer and its importance in data transmission.
Day 8 Layer 5: Session Layer Overview of the Session Layer and its functions.
Day 9 Layer 6: Presentation Layer Introduction to the Presentation Layer and its role in data formatting.
Day 10 Layer 7: Application Layer Explanation of the Application Layer and its interaction with end-user applications.
Day 11 What is an IP Address? Introduction to IP addresses, including IPv4 and IPv6.
Day 12 Subnetting Basics Explanation of subnetting and its importance in network management.
Day 13 Introduction to VLANs Overview of Virtual Local Area Networks (VLANs) and their benefits.
Day 14 What is Routing? Basic concepts of routing and its role in network communication.
Day 15 Types of Routing Protocols Introduction to different types of routing protocols and their uses.
Day 16 What is Switching? Explanation of switching and its importance in network connectivity.
Day 17 Introduction to ACLs Overview of Access Control Lists (ACLs) and their role in network security.
Day 18 Basic Network Security Introduction to basic network security concepts and best practices.
Day 19 Network Troubleshooting Tools Overview of common network troubleshooting tools and their uses.
Day 20 CCNA Certification Tips Tips and best practices for preparing and passing the CCNA certification exam.
